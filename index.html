CALL TO ACTION

What can we do as students?

#Teamtrees: donate $1 to plant a tree

Forest App: plant trees while studying

Help you stay focused while you study and a virtual tree grows during this period. If you use other softwares during this period, the virtual tree dies. If the virtual tree grew up, you will harvest a tree and coins. A real tree will be planted in real life if you collect enough virtual coins.

Let's Go Paperless!

Go paperless. Recycle and buy recycled products. Try take notes on electronic devices. Since one of the core material to make paper is wood. Go paperless will reduce the the consumption of paper. Hence, reduce the consumption of trees.
